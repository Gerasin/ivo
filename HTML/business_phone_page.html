Business Phone

Get your business started with a business phone number from IVO. Your phone number from IVO offers all the communication features you'll ever need, including call forwarding, auto attendant, voice mail, e-fax. Or, if you're always on the go, get the ShoreTel Sky professional mobile application together with your business phone number and make calls from anywhere in the world with an internet connection. Any calls made from your mobile app will show the call being made from your listed IVO business phone number, not your personal number. With this convenient mobile app you can also manage your telephone profile (VoIP system) account from just about anywhere.

Basic Business Phone
Automated Attendant Services

- Auto attendant (AA). Allows callers to be transferred to an extension based on their selection from a menu, without help from a live operator or receptionist.
- Dial by number. Auto-attendant menu option that enables callers to reach a user by dialing his or her number.

Call Forwarding

- Call forwarding. Forwards incoming calls to an alternate phone number.
- Find Me/Follow me call routing. Find Me lists are more sophisticated than Call Forwarding; e.g. a user can configure it to first ring his office phone, then after a number of unanswered rings, to ring his cellphone, then his home phone.

Voicemail & Messaging

- Voicemail box. Allows callers to leave a message in a voicemail box. This voicemail box is normally accessible by the receptionist and greets callers with information specific to your company
- Voicemail to email WAV file forwarding. Sends a notification with the WAV sound file attached to any email address

Caller ID, Redial, Hold & other Features

- Caller ID with Name. A service that displays the caller’s name on the calling party’s digital readout. This is similar to Caller ID except that the calling party’s name is displayed along with the calling number or instead of the calling number
- Hold. A user may pause the current conversation, and retrieve the call from the same phone
- Redial. A user can call the previous internal or external phone number dialed without re-entering the number

Fax-to-email service

- Incoming faxes are received in your e-mail inbox. Outbound faxes are sent as email attachments
- Preferred solution if you want to eliminate paper and send/receive faxes anywhere you access your e-mail

Conferencing

- Conferencing floor Control/Moderator. A user can set up a conference call with up to five additional parties, serve as moderator for a conference call, managing invitations, viewing and resetting “hand-raises,” muting individual participants, and more...
- On-Screen Moderator Conference Controls. A user can manage conference participants from a conference service menu on the cellphone

Business Phone Mobility
Mobility client app

- Take and receive business calls on your favorite mobile device using your Business Phone Number. The ShoreTel Sky mobility solution selects the best available network (Wi/Fi or 3G/4G) to ensure great voice quality. Enjoy enterprise phone system features and stay connected wherever you are.
- Remote Phone Use. Remote and traveling users can use a phone number from any location with a internet connection, such as a home office
- Mobility Client Specs
- iOS specs

- Android specs
- BlackBerry Specs
- Symbian / Nokia Specs

Telecom & Portability

800 numbers / Toll Free numbers. Toll free numbers let customers call you free of charge. Vanity toll free numbers let you brand your phone number.

Call Recording

- Record: Our Receptionist will do Call recording for all calls.
- Playback: Recordings will be send to your email.
