The leading Virtual Office provider!

IVO provides cost-effective virtual office services for growing businesses and professionals seeking to expand their businesses without spending the exorbitant funds required for expensive full-time offices. We’ve been providing virtual office services since 1995, and provide the most advanced, responsive virtual office solutions, including U.S. Mail services, Live Receptionist Services and Executive Office Space. In addition to virtual offices, we also provide a host of other services to compliment your growing business, including professional legal services, accounting services, secretarial services, web site development and other business development and marketing services.

Our virtual office center is located at 2372 Morse Avenue, Irvine, California 92614, in the heart of Orange County’s financial district. Stop by for a visit, enjoy a cup of coffee and meet our virtual office team. You’ll find our staff to be pleasant and professional.

Having a virtual office in the center of Orange County, California can provide a big boost for your business, and will allow you to leverage your time and effectiveness, increase your exposure and expand your profits.
